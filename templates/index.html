About GLH

Greenfield Local Hub is a cooperative marketplace built to bridge the gap between local farmers and the communities they feed. We believe that food tastes better when you know where it comes from — when it's grown with care, harvested fresh, and delivered without the middleman. Our platform connects you directly with independent producers across your region, so every purchase you make supports a real farm, a real family, and a more sustainable food system. Whether you're a customer looking for honest, fresh produce or a farmer ready to bring your harvest to more tables, Greenfield Local Hub is your home.
